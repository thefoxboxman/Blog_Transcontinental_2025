Nullabor Crossing

Today and for the next few days we are headed east across the "Nullabor". From Norseman to Ceduna. A 1201km drive with nothing but roadhouses punctuating the wildneress.

The road is wide, flat and straigt. One 146km (90 mile) section is the longest straight section of road in Australia

For two days we drive through the Great Western Woodland. Now that's a forest.

We plan to do the drive in 3 days. Our first overnight stop is at the Cocklebiddy Roadhouse.

The next morning we go down our first hill as we come down the escarpment at Madura. The escarpement runs the entire length across the Great Australian Bight. At Madura the highway goes down on to the coastal plain.

Later in the day we go back up the escarpement at Eucla. Eucla is about 20km west of the Western Australia - South Australia border.

After nearly two days drivimng we get our first glimpe of the Great Australian Bight and the cliffs.

Now in South Australia and we are traveling beside the magnificant cliffs of the Great Australian Bight.
